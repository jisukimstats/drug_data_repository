# Malaysia Crime Report 2024: Drug Statistics Indicators

This document categorizes the available data points (2021-2023) from the Department of Statistics Malaysia (Source: NADA) into **Supply Reduction** and **Demand Reduction** indicators.

---

## 1. Supply Reduction (Law Enforcement)
Indicators related to police enforcement, arrests, and cases aiming to disrupt the illegal drug supply chain.

* **Number of drug cases and arrests by state**
* **Number of drug arrests by age group and sex**

---

## 2. Demand Reduction (User Identification & Rehabilitation)
Indicators focusing on the profiles of drug users, addicts, and the services provided to reduce the desire and need for drugs.

### A. Profile of Identified Drug Addicts
Statistics regarding individuals identified as drug addicts, categorized by various demographic and socioeconomic factors.

* **Geographical Distribution**
    * Drug addicts by state and sex
    * Drug addicts by administrative district
* **Demographics**
    * Drug addicts by state and age group
    * Drug addicts by state and ethnic group
* **Socioeconomic Status**
    * Drug addicts by state and academic qualification
    * Drug addicts by state and type of occupation
* **Substance Type**
    * Drug addicts by state and type of drug addiction
* **Detection & Facilities**
    * Number of National Anti-Drugs Agency (NADA) facilities and drug addicts detected by type of services

### B. Treatment and Rehabilitation Clients
Statistics related to individuals actively undergoing treatment and rehabilitation programs.

* **Program Participation**
    * Clients receiving treatment and rehabilitation programme by state
    * Clients receiving treatment and rehabilitation programme by case status
* **Client Demographics**
    * Clients receiving treatment and rehabilitation programme by sex
    * Clients receiving treatment and rehabilitation programme by age group
* **Addiction Profile in Treatment**
    * Clients receiving treatment and rehabilitation programme by type of drug addiction
