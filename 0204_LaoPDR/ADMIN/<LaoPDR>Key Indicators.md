# Report of Seizure of Illicit Drugs: Lao PDR (2019-2023)

This document outlines the key drug-related statistical indicators available in the "Report of Seizure of Illicit Drugs," published by the **Lao National Commission for Drug Control and Supervision (LCDC)**. The data covers the period from **2019 to 2023**.

**Data Source:** Lao National Commission for Drug Control and Supervision (LCDC)

---

## 1. Supply Reduction (Drug Seizures)
Indicators tracking the quantity and types of illicit substances seized by authorities to disrupt the illegal drug market.

* **Amphetamine-Type Stimulants (ATS)**
    * ATS (Methamphetamine Tablets/Yaba)
    * Ice (Crystal Methamphetamine)
    * Powder (Methamphetamine Powder)
* **Opioids**
    * Heroin
    * Opium
* **Other Substances**
    * Cannabis
    * Cocaine

---

## 2. Law Enforcement (Offender Statistics)
Statistics regarding individuals apprehended for drug-related offenses, providing insight into the demographics and trends of drug crime.

* **Temporal Trends**
    * Total offenders by Month
* **Demographics**
    * Total offenders by gender
* **Transnational Crime**
    * Foreign offenders by Country
