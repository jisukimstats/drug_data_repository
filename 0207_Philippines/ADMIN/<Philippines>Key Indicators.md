# Philippine Drug Enforcement Agency (PDEA) Reports Summary (2019-2024)

This document outlines the key drug-related statistical indicators consistently reported in the Annual and Quarterly Reports published by the **Philippine Drug Enforcement Agency (PDEA)**. The data covers the period from **2019 to the 2nd Quarter of 2024**.

**Data Source:** Philippine Drug Enforcement Agency (PDEA)

---

## 1. Supply Reduction (Law Enforcement)
Indicators related to anti-illegal drug operations, arrests, and seizures aimed at disrupting the drug supply chain.

### A. Operational Achievements
* **Total Anti-Drug Operations:** Number of operations conducted by PDEA and other law enforcement agencies (OLEAs).
* **Total Arrested Personalities:** Total number of individuals arrested for drug-related offenses.
* **High-Value Targets (HVTs) Arrested:** Specific count of high-profile arrests, including government officials, elected officials, uniformed personnel, foreign nationals, and drug group leaders/members.
* **Dismantling of Drug Facilities:**
    * Clandestine Laboratories dismantled.
    * Drug Dens / Tiangge dismantled.

### B. Drug Seizures (Volume & Value)
Quantities and estimated value of dangerous drugs seized.

* **Total Value of Seized Drugs:** Monetary value in Philippine Peso (Php).
* **Methamphetamine Hydrochloride (Shabu):** Total volume seized (kg/grams).
* **Marijuana:**
    * Dried Leaves (kg).
    * Plants/Stalks (pieces).
    * Seeds (kg/grams).
* **Ecstasy (MDMA):** Number of tablets/capsules or volume seized.
* **Cocaine:** Total volume seized.

### C. Barangay Drug Clearing Program (BDCP)
A unique Philippine indicator tracking the status of drug-affected villages (Barangays).

* **Total Drug-Affected Barangays:** Number of barangays identified as affected.
* **Drug-Cleared Barangays:** Number of barangays declared "Drug-Cleared" after intervention.
* **Drug-Free Barangays:** Number of barangays maintaining drug-free status.

---

## 2. Demand Reduction (Prevention & Reformation)
Strategies aimed at reducing the public's desire for illegal drugs through education and rehabilitation programs.

### A. Preventive Education and Community Involvement (PECI)
* **Total Activities Conducted:** Number of seminars, lectures, and symposia held.
* **Audience Reach:** Total number of individuals reached through educational activities.
    * *Target Groups:* Students, Educators, Workplaces, Communities/Barangays.

### B. Treatment and Reformation
* **Balay Silangan:** The national community-based reformation program.
    * Number of Balay Silangan Reformation Centers established.
    * Number of clients/reformists served or graduated.
* **Drug-Free Workplace Program:** Number of government and private offices conducting drug-free workplace policies and authorized drug testing.

---

## 3. Regional Specifics (Mindanao Drug Threat Assessment)
Specific indicators reported for regional analysis (e.g., 2024 Mindanao Report).

* **Drug Prices:** Street prices of Shabu and Marijuana by region.
* **Trafficking Routes:** Identification of entry points (seaports, airports, coastlines) and transshipment points.
* **Profile of Drug Personalities:** Demographic data of arrestees in the specific region.
